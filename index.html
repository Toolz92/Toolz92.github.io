Exorcism At Duskridge Manor

This is the game I helped develope at Nocturnal Knights. It is a two player third-person assometrical game of cat and mouse. With one player acting as a detective trying to solve the mysteries of the manor, and the other acting as the ghost attempting to drive the detective insane. The detective must search the house looking for evidence and once 5 pieces have been found head towards the final alter to banish the ghost. The ghost must drive off the detective by getting close enoguh to the detective to drain his sainty, and the ghost must drain all the sanity before the detetive can accomplish his goal. Both players have a veriety of resaurces and tools to hinder the other, such as locking doors or placing repelling wards.
